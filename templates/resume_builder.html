AI Resume Builder

This tool helps you create an ATS-optimized resume tailored to a specific job. You will choose a resume template, either upload an existing resume or enter your information manually, then provide a job description. The system will use that information to create a polished resume and generate a PDF download.

Before You Start

  • Have your current resume ready, if you have one.
  • Have the job description for the role you want to apply to.
  • Be ready to provide education, work history, certifications, tools, and languages.
